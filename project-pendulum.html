Pendulum Friction Testing Apparatus

SolidWorks Tribology Research

Research engineering at the Bioengineering Laboratory, Orthopedics Department at Rhode Island Hospital — building an apparatus to measure friction in biological joints.

Overview

  • Designing active and passive pendulum testing apparatus in SolidWorks to measure coefficients of friction in biological joints.
  • Fabricating and assembling an experimental testing device in the RIH Orthopaedic Foundation testing facility.
  • Conducting mechanical validation to characterize the tribological properties of soft tissue structures under physiological loading conditions.

Process

1

Design

Modeling active and passive pendulum configurations in SolidWorks for joint friction measurement.

2

Fabrication & assembly

Building the rig in the RIH Orthopaedic Foundation testing facility.

3

Validation

Characterizing tribological behavior of soft tissue under physiological loading.
