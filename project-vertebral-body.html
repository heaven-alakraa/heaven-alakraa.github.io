Next-Gen Material Selection — Vertebral Body Replacement

Ansys Granta FEA Biomedical

A material selection case report identifying next-generation candidates for vertebral body replacement (VBR) implants.

Overview

I translated clinical needs and finite element analysis results into quantitative design criteria, then screened thousands of materials to find the best fit.

  • Translated clinical needs and FEA results into quantitative mechanical and biological design criteria.
  • Screened 4,395 materials in Ansys Granta Edupack and the ASM Medical Materials databases.
  • Identified Polyetherimide (PEI) as a leading candidate for future clinical application based on mechanical and biocompatibility properties.

Process

1

Define requirements

Set mechanical (stiffness, strength, fatigue) and biological (biocompatibility, imaging compatibility) constraints from clinical context and FEA.

2

Database screening

Filtered 4,395 candidates in Ansys Granta Edupack and ASM Medical Materials against the design criteria.

3

Ranking & selection

Compared finalists on performance indices and identified PEI as the leading next-generation candidate.
