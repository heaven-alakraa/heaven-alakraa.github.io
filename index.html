About

I'm a mechanical engineering student at Brown University eager to solve problems across multiple fields, spanning from biomedical devices to infrastructure, from robotics to automotive and aerospace.

Outside of classes, I've worked across research labs, biotech startups, and teaching roles, building projects that push me to apply theory to physical prototypes.
