RNA Purification Tube Redesign

SolidWorks 3D Printing Biotech

A product design project at Lilac Biosciences Inc. to improve RNA purification workflows by replacing centrifuge-dependent separation with integrated filtration.

Overview

  • Redesigning RNA purification tube geometry in SolidWorks to replace centrifuge-dependent separation with integrated filtration.
  • Fabricating and iterating prototypes via 3D printing for experimental validation.
  • Evaluating filtration membrane candidates through structured literature review and vendor analysis to identify optimal pore size and material compatibility for RNA retention.

Process

1

Problem definition

Mapped the limitations of centrifuge-dependent RNA separation and set targets for an integrated-filtration alternative.

2

Geometry design

Modeled candidate tube geometries in SolidWorks around the chosen filtration approach.

3

Membrane evaluation

Reviewed literature and vendor options to select pore size and material compatible with RNA retention.

4

Prototype & iterate

3D printing prototypes for experimental validation and design iteration.
